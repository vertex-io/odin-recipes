Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Preheat pan on stovetop
  2. Add oil to pan
  3. Crack eggs and add to pan
  4. Cook until desired
  5. Carefully flip eggs with spatula
  6. Remove eggs from pan when done!
